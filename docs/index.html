How it works

The Pomodoro
Technique, perfected.

A time-tested method used by millions. Work in focused sprints, take real breaks, and watch your productivity soar.

01
Pick your task

Choose what you'll work on from your to-do list or just start fresh with a focus label.

02
Work for 25 minutes

Deep, uninterrupted focus. The timer runs in the notification shade so you're never tempted to check the time.

03
Take a real break

5 minutes to breathe, stretch, or listen to music. The app handles the timing so you actually rest.

04
Track your progress

Each completed session is logged. Watch your focus time accumulate and your habits solidify.
